About Us

At Red Rock Country Swing, we celebrate the joy of country swing dance by bringing people together through music, movement, and community. Whether you're a beginner taking your first step or a seasoned dancer looking to connect, our goal is to create an inclusive space full of laughter, rhythm, and unforgettable memories.

Based in the heart of Southern Utah, we host regular socials, lessons, and community events in both St. George and Washington. With passionate instructors and a welcoming vibe, Red Rock Country Swing is more than just dance — it's a lifestyle.
